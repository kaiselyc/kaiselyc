The Book of Love
by: Risen

Happy Valentines to the love of my life!

At first, I was panicking. I have been staring at this website for a while now, trying to figure out the perfect way to ask you something that feels both simple and important. I went back and forth on what to make, what to give, how to turn this into the kind of Valentine’s invitation that would make you smile before you even finished reading it. And somewhere between overthinking and rewriting, I realized something: the only thing that really matters is you. So here it is, simple.

Here are more memories of us! :)

I remember the time...

This is our 2nd valentines together, and I remember the time that I was so nervous about inviting you in our first valentines, and now I won't even flinch, because now, I am serious. I am truly yours, and you are truly mine. So today, and every day, I celebrate you, us, and the love we are growing together. But honestly, I don't just celebrate valentines this month, I celebrate every memory we’ve made, every moments we have exchanged, every conversation, and even the challenges we’ve faced, because they’ve only made us stronger. You are my favorite story, my greatest adventure, and my most treasured gift, and I can’t wait to create countless more memories together.








My love, will you be my valentines?
